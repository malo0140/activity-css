Bioshock

Information

Bioshock is the genetically enhanced first person shooter that lets you do things never before possible in the genre: turn everything into a weapon, biologically mod your body with plasmids, hack devises and systems, upgrade your weapons and craft new ammo variants, and experiment with diffrent battle techniques in an increadible and unique underwater city.

You are a cast-away in Rapture, an underwater Utopia torn apart by civil war. Caught between powerful forces, and hunted down by genetically modified splicers and deadly securtity systems, you have to come to grips with a deadly, mysterious world filled with powerful technology and fascinating characters. No encounter ever plays out the same, and no two gamers will play the game the same.

Here are just a few things you can do to a foe:

  • Catch his grenades in mid air and toss them back at him
  • Freeze him solid and shatter him with smack of your wrench
  • Lead him and his comrade to the water and zap them all with 1000 Volts
  • Take research photos of him to learn his weaknesses
